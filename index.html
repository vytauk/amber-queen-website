Amber Queen

Her coronation coming soon

“It is better to dance in freedom than march for war.”

Amber Queen is a captivating novel by Vytas Aukštuolis that interweaves Baltic mythology with contemporary themes, offering profound insights into today’s uncertain world. Set against a rich tapestry of ancient legends and timeless wisdom, the story delves into themes of power rooted in harmony with each other and the natural world, contrasting it with power derived from violence and domination.

Through vivid storytelling and deeply relatable characters, Amber Queen bridges the gap between myth and modernity, inviting readers on a journey of discovery, reflection, and hope.
